\question Evaluate the integral

\[
\iint_{R} (xy + x^2) \, dA,
\]

\question Do these two integrals evaluate to the same thing or not? Explain. 

\[
\int_0^9 \int_{-2}^4 x^2 e^{xy \sin(x)} \, dx \, dy \hspace{3cm}  \int_{-2}^4 \int_{0}^9 z^2 e^{xz \sin(z)} \, dx \, dz
\]
